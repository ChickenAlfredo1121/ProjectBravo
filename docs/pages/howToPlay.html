How to Play!

Thank you for playing my game! The goal of the game is to deliver mail! You are a mail man/woman and your goal and to deliver your stack of mail to the right houses as quick as possible! Good luck! How to Play:

  1. Click and drag the newspaper to the house with the same number!
  2. Repeat for all 6 newspapers
  3. Once you've delievered all the mail, your time will appear!
  4. Try and get the fastest time, but be warned the houses change order!
  5. Thank you for playing!
