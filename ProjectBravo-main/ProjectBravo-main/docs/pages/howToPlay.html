How to Play!

Thank you for playing my game! The goal of the game is to deliver mail! You are a paper boy that is going house-to-house delivering mail. The more mail you deliver the higher your score will be! If you deliver all the mail a new load of mail will appear! How to Play:

  1. click and drag the boy to the stack of papers
  2. click and drag boy to houses, releasing on each house to drop off the paper
  3. once you've delievered mail to your hearts content, hit submit
  4. score will apear!
  5. Thank you for playing!
